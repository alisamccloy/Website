About

The person behind the frame.

I am a Canterbury-based photographer and film-maker working across editorial stills and short-form motion. My work is built around restraint, observation, and a strong sense of mood.

I photograph portraits and editorial stories for magazines, brands, and independent collaborators, and I approach film with the same care for light, rhythm, and quiet detail.

Currently open to assisting, commissions, and collaboration. If the tone of the work feels close to what you are making, I would like to hear from you.
