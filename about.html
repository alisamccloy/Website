The Person
Behind the Frame

I’m a Canterbury-based photographer and film-maker in my final year of study, working across editorial stills and short-form motion. My work sits where documentary instinct meets a designed frame — quiet, considered, and built to hold attention without raising its voice.

I shoot portraits and editorial stories for magazines, brands, and independent collaborators, and direct short films with the same eye for light and restraint. Whether it’s a single frame or a finished cut, I care most about mood, composition, and getting people to look like themselves.

Currently open to assisting, commissions, and collaboration. If we share a sensibility, I’d like to hear from you.
